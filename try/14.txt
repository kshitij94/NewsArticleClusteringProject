When the 57th Grammy Awards take place in Los Angeles on Sunday night, viewers at home will be checking in on an annual convocation that consistently fails to declare its true purpose. Are the Grammys an elite ceremony honoring artistic excellence? Or is musics biggest night just a televised industry party celebrating commercial popularity? Music fans have every right to be vexed over the meaning of the Grammys and this year, our anxieties over which art will be celebrated (and which art wont be) seem to parallel our greater national anxieties about race.
